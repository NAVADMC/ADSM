NAADSM Training

The NAADSM training materials were developed for in-person epidemiological simulation modeling workshops offered in 2007 and 2008. These materials are provided as reference materials only. In some cases, working examples are referenced. It is not possible to provide all example materials as the file types are not supported.

The NAADSM training materials were funded in part by cooperative agreements with the USDA.
